About Us.

This submarine sandwich restaurant specialises in all sorts of goodness regarding sandwiches. With various bread options and fillings, customers can customise their sandwiches to their liking. Some of the popular options have been added to the sub of the month category, there you can buy any of the most popular sandwiches from the last month. In addition to sandwiches, Sub Haus also has a special Haus sauce which is added to all sandwiches. This sauce is vegan and gluten-free.
